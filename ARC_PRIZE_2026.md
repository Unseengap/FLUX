# ARC Prize 2026 - Kaggle Competitions

Here are the details for the three distinct tracks/competitions for the ARC Prize 2026 on Kaggle:

## 1. ARC Prize 2026 - ARC-AGI-2
**URL:** [https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/arc-prize-2026-arc-agi-2](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/arc-prize-2026-arc-agi-2)

**Description:** 
This competition focuses on building models that can adapt and solve novel intelligence tests from the ARC (Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus) dataset. It challenges participants to achieve fundamental generalization and human-level abstraction capabilities.

## 2. ARC Prize 2026 - ARC-AGI-3
**URL:** [https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/arc-prize-2026-arc-agi-3](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/arc-prize-2026-arc-agi-3)

**Description:** 
This is another main competition tier in the 2026 series, challenging AI researchers to improve their solvers to score higher on hidden, rigorous ARC reasoning benchmarks in the pursuit of AGI (Artificial General Intelligence).

## 3. ARC Prize 2026 - Paper Track
**URL:** [https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/arc-prize-2026-paper-track](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/arc-prize-2026-paper-track)

**Description:** 
A dedicated track for submitting formal research papers, write-ups, and methodologies. This allows for qualitative, theoretical contributions and novel architectural approaches (rather than pure code submissions) to be systematically shared with the broader AI research community.
