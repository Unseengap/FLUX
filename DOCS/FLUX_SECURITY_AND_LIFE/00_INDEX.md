## Key Principles

### 1. Opt-In Everything
Every sharing feature requires explicit consent. Default = maximum privacy.

### 2. Personal FLUX Sovereignty
Your FLUX is an extension of your cognition. You cannot be locked out of your own FLUX. Fifth Amendment protection applies.

### 3. Distributed Witness Network
Other people's FLUX instances can record you in their spaces. You have no Fifth Amendment claim to suppress evidence from someone else's "memory."

### 4. Human Dignity Override
FLUX protects human life, not just its owner's interests. Crimes against non-users still trigger evidence preservation.

### 5. Open Format
The `.flx` format is fully inspectable. No black boxes. Security researchers can audit every tensor.
